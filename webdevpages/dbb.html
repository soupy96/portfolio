Design By Blackridge

Created in 2019 for Blackridge Strategy

Blackridge Strategy was wanting a website that showcased their Digital Design Tools and Resources to clients to show that they do more than just GR and PR. Thus Design By Blackridge was made, by me! It consisted of mainly a static page with some parallax effects added. The site was made through Wordpress, the WPBakery plugin and many others to achieve the final product. While using Wordpress and the WPBakery plugin did make responsive development easier for the most part it did make it a pain to tweak little details of the website.

Its goal was to get future clients to see who Design By Blackridge was and what they could offer. To be able to see what we were capable of and contact us for business with a contact form below. I included on-scroll animation effects that allowed sections of the page to slide, fade or glide onto the webpage.

You can see below the animations that were implemented on the card system. We wanted something other than just a static site so we decided for the cards to slide in and then the logo to fade ontop of the cards itsself. 2/3 cards lead to sites that I developed while I was working at Blackridge Strategy as well,you can see them here: Jumpsuit, Updraft.

A simple contact form that was created via Ninja Forms was implemented via shortcode. This was my first time working with Ninja forms and it made my life MUCH easier! Ninja forms made it easy and simple to create a contact form while keeping the design and appeal of the website in brand.
